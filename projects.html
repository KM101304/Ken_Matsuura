Community

No Code Coding Club (N3C)
Community  ·  May 2025 – Jul 2025  ·  Capilano University

The pitch was simple: you don't need to know how to code to build software anymore. AI changed that. N3C was built around that idea — bringing vibe-code-driven hackathons to Capilano University, where most students come from business, design, and arts backgrounds, not CS degrees. The goal was to show that with the right tools and a willingness to build, anyone could ship something real in a weekend.

We ran weekly build sessions and a hackathon at CapU. Three months. Short run, real lessons about organizing from scratch, getting people to show up, and keeping momentum in a community that didn't exist before you started it.
